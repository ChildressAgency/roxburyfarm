About

Roxbury Farm & Garden

Everything for your lawn and garden.

We have an extensive line of trees, shrubs, perennials, bedding plants & bulbs. Our selection is second to none.

When it comes to having a beautiful lawn, we offer the latest in turf type Tall Fescues as well as any type of specialty grass seeds. We understand the life cycle of grasses, and Roxbury will advise you for successful results. Our knowledge of proper fertilization yields green grass in an environmentally responsible manner.

Roxbury Farm & Garden Center has been catering to the organic gardener for over 25 years. We have many natural products to suit your needs.

Roxbury started as a feed store in 1929 and we still carry grains such as corn, wheat and oats. Our complete line of Nutrena livestock feeds offers the finest nutritional science available for the optimum health of your animals.

We also cater to contractors for erosion controls such as a straw and excelsior blankets. We carry the seed, fertilizer, lime and hand tools needed to get the job done.

Roxbury also offers seed for pasture and wildlife such as orchardgrass, clover, buckwheat, and millet to name a few.

When it comes to weed, insect and disease control Roxbury is the place to go. We know how our products work, so you'll benefit from accurate advice geared to our locality.

Roxbury has many specialty lines including wine & beer making supplies, water gardening items, and bulk vegetable seed.

We have devoted much time to training our staff employees. Feel free to bring your questions and ideas to us. We can help solve your problems and point you in the right direction.

Oh yeah... Special order are welcome!

Roxbury Farm & Garden Center looks forward to serving you.

Andy Lynn
