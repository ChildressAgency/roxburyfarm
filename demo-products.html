Lawn Care

Roxbury Farm & Garden Center is the place for lawn care products. We feature professional products and top variety grass seeds including Winning Colors Tall Fescue and the legendary Roxbury Lawn Grass Mix.

For over 30 years we have been leading customers to success in growing grass in a tough climate. We tend to be a little far north for Southern grasses and a little far south for Northern grasses.

By the late 1980's the folks at Roxbury Farm & Garden Center understood that proper timing and dosage of fertilization was curcial in creating a balance between a nice lawn and a healthy Cheasapeake Bay.

A soil test is important in developing a plan of action. We recommend sending a sample to Virginia Tech and we generally keep their soil test collection box at the store. There is a charge for a soil test payable to Virginia Tech.

Pet and Livestock Feed

Our pet food lines include Chicken Soup Pet Lover's Soul cat and dog food. These holistic pet foods are made from natural ingredients to ensure the maximum health of your pet.

Nutrena Animal feeds offer nutrition based on advanced nutritional science. Research farms, with teams of PhD nutritionalists, work diligently to provide your livestock with the best diet possible for horses, poultry, swine, and ruminants.

We also process our own locally grown corn to produce cracked corn, scratch grain, and our legendary wild bird blend. Additionally we have wheat, oats & bran.

Trees, Shrubs & Other Plants

The Nursery at Roxbury Farm and Garden Center is open year round. We feature a great selection of bedding plants, perennials, roses and ground covers.

Our extensive line of trees, shrubs and evergreens are sure to meet your landscaping needs. Roxbury carries a wonderful variety of evergreens, fruit trees and flowering trees.

If azaleas, roses, or boxwoods are your passion then you need ot come see us!

Don't forget, Christmas is a great time to visit Roxbury, to choose from a large selection of cut or live Christmas trees, live roping and wreaths.

Bird & Wildlife Products

Roxbury carries its own blend of wild bird seed mix in various sizes, along with Nyger seed, Safflower seed, Striped, Black Oil, and Sunflower kernels. For other wild creatures, Roxbury carries dried corn on the cob, peanuts and salt blocks.

Roxbury is a supplier and authorized repair facility for the Droll Yankee brand of Bird Feeders, but many other brands of feeders, houses (including bat houses), hummingbird feeders and bird feeding supplies are available.
